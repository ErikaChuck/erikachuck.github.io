Evaluation of the A/B Test for an Enhanced Recommendation System

A new recommendation system was evaluated through an A/B test, analyzing its impact on funnel conversion and providing recommendations based on statistically significant results.

An in-depth analysis was conducted on an A/B test designed to evaluate the effectiveness of a new recommendation system for an international online store. The test, named recommender_system_test, compared the current checkout funnel (Group A) with the optimized funnel (Group B) over the period from December 7, 2020, to January 1, 2021, involving 15% of new users from the EU region.

The objective was to determine if users showed improved conversion rates across key funnel stages (product_page → product_cart → purchase) within the first 14 days after registration, aiming for a 10% increase at each stage..

The analysis involved detailed data cleaning and exploration, identification of potential anomalies, and evaluation of the test’s validity through statistical techniques such as z-tests to assess significant differences between groups. The results provided critical insights into the recommendation system's effectiveness and its impact on user behavior.
