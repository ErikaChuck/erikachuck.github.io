Identifying Inefficient Operators in CallMeMaybe

Data from CallMeMaybe was analyzed to identify inefficient operators and validate differences through statistical tests. Recommendations were made to optimize call distribution and improve operational efficiency.

This project focused on analyzing data from the CallMeMaybe virtual telephony service to identify inefficient operators and propose improvement strategies. An exploratory data analysis was conducted to understand patterns of incoming, outgoing, and internal calls, and key metrics such as missed calls and wait times were determined.

Based on these metrics, operators performing below the expected standard were identified. Statistical hypotheses were tested to validate differences between efficient and inefficient operators using t-tests and ANOVA.

The findings enabled the development of practical recommendations to optimize call distribution and improve operational efficiency.
